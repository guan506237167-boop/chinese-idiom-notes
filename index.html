Chinese Idiom Learning Resources

Chinese idioms are short expressions with stories behind them. For learners, a story can make a four-character idiom easier to remember than a plain word list.

This page collects simple resources for learning Chinese idioms, their meanings, and the cultural lessons behind them.

Chengyu Practice Quiz

A quiz can be a useful way to check whether learners really understand a Chinese idiom, not just its English translation.

For chengyu learning, a simple process works well: read the story first, guess the meaning from context, check the English explanation, and then answer a short quiz question.

This method is useful because many Chinese idioms are compact. The four characters often carry a story, a cultural image, and a practical meaning at the same time.

For learners who want a quick review activity, this Chinese idiom quiz can be used after reading a few short idiom stories.
